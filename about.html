About

Hello, and Welcome to My First PC. My name is Carlos. I created this website because I wanted to bridge the gap between PC building and your average consumer. My goal is to equip customers with the knowledge, and confidence to build their first PC which will save them money, and give them a product they're truly happy with.

My first PC is a brand new initiative that started with just one person, and by word of mouth had evolved into a small community of like-minded PC builders. and all of us are constantly sharing new information and technologies that are released to bring you the most up-to-date knowledge.

before 2015 I remember always spending money on overpriced gaming laptops, and desktops. Meanwhile, I already had friends that were experimenting with changing out PC parts from their prebuilt desktops. At the time I remember feeling intimidated, and uncertain that I could build a desktop. I thought "You'd have to be a genius to do that" but after 2015 I realized just how easy it was, and found it to be very similar to the Legos, and K'nex toys that children play with growing up. When I saw the result of my labor, I realized I never wanted to buy a prebuilt desktop again.

Here at My First PC, we want to give you knowledge and confidence. to guide you along the path to an enjoyable PC experience, at a price that you can adjust. and we will continue to work to give you the best experience possible, and leave you wondering "why didn't I do this sooner?"
